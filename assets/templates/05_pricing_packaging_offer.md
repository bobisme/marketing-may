## Pricing page checklist

- [ ] Clear value metric.
- [ ] Buyer can estimate bill before signup.
- [ ] Packages map to recognizable use cases.
- [ ] Upgrade trigger is obvious.
- [ ] Support/onboarding expectations are clear.
- [ ] Usage limits and overages are plain-language.
- [ ] Refund/cancellation terms are visible.
- [ ] Enterprise/custom pricing reason is explained.
- [ ] FAQ handles budget, switching, security, and usage concerns.
