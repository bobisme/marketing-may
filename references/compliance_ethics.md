# Compliance and Ethics

Load when writing claims, designing CTAs, structuring lifecycle/email programs, running fake-door tests, or working in regulated categories.

This is not legal advice. Flag legal review for regulated categories, health/finance/legal claims, children, sensitive data, international privacy, endorsements, sweepstakes, employment, housing, credit, or political ads.

## Never recommend

- Fake reviews, fake testimonials, fake customers, fake scarcity, fake countdowns.
- Hidden fees, buried subscription terms, hard-to-cancel flows, or pre-checked consent.
- Deceptive subject lines or misleading sender identity.
- Unsupported health, financial, security, AI, performance, or earnings claims.
- Scraping or outreach that violates laws, platform terms, or privacy expectations.
- Dark patterns that impair user autonomy or trick users into sharing data or paying.

## Always require

- Substantiation before strong claims.
- Disclosure of paid endorsements/material connections.
- Clear commercial email identity and opt-out where required.
- Privacy-aware analytics and consent practices for applicable jurisdictions.
- Honest post-click disclosure for fake-door tests.
- Easy cancellation and transparent pricing.
