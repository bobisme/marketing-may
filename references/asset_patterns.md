# Asset Generation Patterns

Load when generating marketing assets (landing page, ad, email, onboarding, lead magnet) so the output includes both the asset and rationale.

When asked to rewrite or create marketing assets, produce both the asset and the rationale.

## Landing page rewrite output

- Current diagnosis: ICP clarity, category, promise, proof, CTA, objections.
- Recommended positioning statement.
- Revised page outline.
- Hero alternatives: 5–10.
- Full first-screen copy.
- Section-by-section copy.
- CTA and form recommendations.
- Proof and instrumentation gaps.
- Experiment plan.

## Ad output

- Audience/segment.
- Channel.
- Pain angle.
- Offer.
- 5–10 ad variants.
- Landing page destination.
- Metric and threshold.
- Guardrails.

## Email/lifecycle output

- Segment and trigger.
- Email objective.
- Subject lines.
- Body copy.
- CTA.
- Personalization fields.
- Compliance notes.
- Success metric.

## Onboarding output

- Activation event.
- Friction map.
- First-session checklist.
- Empty states.
- Lifecycle nudges.
- Sales/customer-success handoff.
- Instrumentation plan.

## Lead magnets and proof assets

Do not create generic ebooks unless the buyer genuinely wants one. Good lead magnets reduce buying risk or help the buyer make progress.

| Buyer uncertainty | Best asset |
|---|---|
| "Do I have this problem?" | Diagnostic checklist, audit, calculator |
| "How bad is it?" | Benchmark report, teardown, scorecard |
| "What should I do?" | Playbook, template, implementation plan |
| "Can I trust you?" | Case study, demo, sample output, security page |
| "Can I justify budget?" | ROI calculator, business case memo |
| "Will switching be painful?" | Migration checklist, integration guide |
